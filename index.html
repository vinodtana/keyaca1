Why Future Business Technologies​

The current & the future of any business is TECHNOLOGY

The current and future success of any business is inextricably linked to the adoption and mastery of cutting-edge technologies.

AI/ML making inroads into manufacturing, finance & healthcare

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are transforming key sectors like manufacturing, finance, and healthcare, driving unprecedented efficiencies and breakthroughs.

APIs enabling integration of multiple business models

Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) are enabling seamless integration between diverse business models, fostering new levels of collaboration and innovation.

100% of the world's industries are experimenting cutting-edge technologies

Across all industries, companies are actively exploring and implementing the latest technological innovations to gain a competitive edge.

Blockchain disrupting the banking, FMCG, Agri-business models

Blockchain technology is revolutionizing traditional business models in banking, consumer goods, and agriculture, creating new opportunities and challenges.

Need for Indian students to be prepared in advance to embrace the change

It is crucial for Indian students to proactively upskill and prepare themselves to navigate the rapidly evolving technological landscape and lead the charge in embracing these transformative changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is Singapore safe for students?

    Yes, Singapore is very safe for students due to its strict laws, efficient law enforcement, robust public safety measures, and clean, orderly environment, making it one of the safest places for students.

  • What is the total cost for this Singapore boot camp?

    The overall cost is Rs. 1,25,000 per student, with no hidden charges.

  • What is included in this boot camp?

    The boot camp includes round-trip flights, visa, accommodation, local transportation in Singapore, university fees, all meals (Indian food), campus visits, city tours, and industry visits.

  • Will you allow caretakers to accompany students?

    Yes, for every batch of 30 students, two faculty members (one male and one female) from the school or College along with PAL caretakers, will accompany the group.

  • How many days does this boot camp require?

    A total of 9 days (7 days in Singapore and 2 days of local college sessions).
